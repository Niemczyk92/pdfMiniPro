Quick guide

  1. Open a PDF
  2. Pick a tool: Add Text or Image
  3. For text: click on the PDF, type with Enter for new lines. Select any part to make it Bold, Italic, Underlined, change color, or remove the white background
  4. Drag any change to move it; double-click text to re-edit; Delete to remove
  5. Save or Print when done. Zoom with the controls in the bottom-right or Ctrl+scroll
